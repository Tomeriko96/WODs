# Arctic Endurance Challenge

**Equipment:** Treadmill or open space
**Time Cap:** 35 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce distance, walk intervals

**Workout:**
- 5-minute warm-up jog
- 8 rounds of:
  - 2 minutes at moderate pace
  - 1 minute at hard pace
- 5-minute cool-down jog

**Notes:** Temperature control training! Focus on maintaining consistent effort through temperature variations. Track total distance.
