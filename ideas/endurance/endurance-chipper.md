# Endurance Chipper

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 20 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce reps, substitute easier movements

**Workout:**
- For time:
  - 100 jumping jacks
  - 80 mountain climbers (each leg)
  - 60 high knees (each leg)
  - 40 burpees
  - 20 sprints (20m each)

**Notes:**
Break up reps as needed. Focus on steady movement.
