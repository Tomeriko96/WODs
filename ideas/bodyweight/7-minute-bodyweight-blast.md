# 7-Minute Bodyweight Blast

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 7 minutes
**Scaling:** Increase rest periods, modify movements to low-impact

**Workout:**
7 minutes AMRAP:
- 7 burpees
- 14 mountain climbers (each leg)
- 21 air squats

**Notes:** Short and intense! Try to maintain pace throughout. Count total rounds completed.
