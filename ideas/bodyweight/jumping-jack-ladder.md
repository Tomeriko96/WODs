# Jumping Jack Ladder

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 12 minutes
**Scaling:** Lower the ladder or reduce push-up reps

**Workout:**
- 10 jumping jacks
- 10 push-ups
- 20 jumping jacks
- 10 push-ups
- 30 jumping jacks
- 10 push-ups
- 40 jumping jacks
- 10 push-ups
- 50 jumping jacks

**Notes:**
Move quickly between sets for a cardio challenge.
