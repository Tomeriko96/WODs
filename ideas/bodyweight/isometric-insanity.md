# Isometric Insanity

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 16 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce hold times or add rest between exercises

**Workout:**
- 4 rounds of:
  - 60 second wall sit
  - 45 second plank hold
  - 30 second single-leg stand (each leg)
  - 20 glute bridges
  - 10 jump squats

**Notes:**
No rest between exercises within a round. Rest 90 seconds between rounds.
