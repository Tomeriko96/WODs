# Mountain Climber Mayhem

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 12 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce rounds or slow down mountain climbers

**Workout:**
- 4 rounds for time:
  - 50 mountain climbers
  - 25 jumping jacks
  - 15 burpees
  - 10 jump squats

**Notes:**
Count mountain climbers as single leg touches. Keep core tight throughout.
