# Bodyweight Medley

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 15 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce total rounds or modify movements to easier variations

**Workout:**
3 rounds for time:
- 100 jumping jacks
- 75 air squats
- 50 push-ups
- 25 burpees

**Notes:**
Classic descending rep scheme with bodyweight staples. Pace yourself on the jumping jacks, the real work starts with the squats.
