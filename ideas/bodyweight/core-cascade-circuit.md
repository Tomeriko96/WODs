# Core Cascade Circuit

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 15 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce hold times, modify exercises to knees

**Workout:**
5 rounds for time:
- 30-second plank
- 20 bicycle crunches (each side)
- 15 leg raises
- 10 pike push-ups
- 5 burpees

**Notes:** Focus on form over speed. Core should be burning by the end!
