# Crawl to Conquer

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 18 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce distance or substitute bear crawls with knee push-ups

**Workout:**
- For time:
  - 400m bear crawl
  - 50 squat thrusts
  - 300m crab walk
  - 40 dive bomber push-ups
  - 200m duck walk
  - 30 jump lunges
  - 100m army crawl

**Notes:**
Break up crawling distances as needed. Focus on maintaining proper form throughout.
