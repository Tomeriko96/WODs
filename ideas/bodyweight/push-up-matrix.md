# Push-Up Matrix

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 15 minutes
**Scaling:** Do knee push-ups or reduce reps

**Workout:**
- 5 rounds for time:
  - 10 push-ups
  - 10 wide push-ups
  - 10 diamond push-ups
  - 10 decline push-ups

**Notes:**
Rest as needed. Focus on full range of motion and hand position changes.
