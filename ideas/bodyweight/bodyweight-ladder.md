# Bodyweight Ladder

**Equipment:** None  
**Time Cap:** 20 minutes
**Scaling:** Start with lower numbers (1-5-1) for beginners

**Workout:**
Ladder up 1-2-3-4-5-4-3-2-1:
- Burpees
- Push-ups  
- Air squats
- Jumping lunges (each leg)

**Notes:** So round 1 is 1 of each, round 2 is 2 of each, etc. Going back down the ladder burns!
