# Plyometric Blast

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 10 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce explosive movements or substitute regular versions

**Workout:**
- 10 minutes AMRAP:
  - 12 jump squats
  - 10 clap push-ups
  - 8 broad jumps
  - 6 tuck jumps
  - 4 burpee broad jumps

**Notes:**
Focus on explosive power. Land softly and reset between jumps.
