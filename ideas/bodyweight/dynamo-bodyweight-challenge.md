# Dynamo Bodyweight Challenge

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 20 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce rounds, modify jump movements to steps

**Workout:**
AMRAP in 20 minutes:
- 100 jumping jacks
- 50 air squats
- 25 push-ups
- 10 burpees
- 5 handstand attempts (or wall walks)

**Notes:** Count completed rounds. Focus on consistent pacing throughout.
