# Kinetic Chain Crusher

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 10 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce reps, break into smaller sets

**Workout:**
For time:
- 50 burpees
- 100 air squats
- 150 mountain climbers
- 200 jumping jacks

**Notes:** One big chipper! Break up reps as needed but keep moving. Full-body conditioning at its finest.
