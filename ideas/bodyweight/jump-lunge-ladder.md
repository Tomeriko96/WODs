# Jump Lunge Ladder

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 10 minutes
**Scaling:** Step-back lunges, reduce ladder height

**Workout:**
- 1 jump lunge per leg, 2 per leg, up to 10 per leg, then back down
- After each set, 10 mountain climbers

**Notes:**
Rest as needed. Focus on soft landings and good form.
