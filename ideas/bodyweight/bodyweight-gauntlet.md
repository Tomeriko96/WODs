# Bodyweight Gauntlet

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 20 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce round count or modify movements

**Workout:**
5 rounds for time:
- 20 air squats
- 15 push-ups
- 10 burpees
- 30-second plank hold
- 40 jumping jacks

**Notes:**
A full-body gauntlet that tests both strength and endurance. Pace yourself early, the volume adds up quickly.
