# Push-Up & Squat Ladder

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 15 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce ladder height or do knee push-ups

**Workout:**
- 1 push-up, 1 squat
- 2 push-ups, 2 squats
- ...
- Up to 10 push-ups, 10 squats, then back down to 1

**Notes:**
Rest as needed. Focus on form and full range of motion.
