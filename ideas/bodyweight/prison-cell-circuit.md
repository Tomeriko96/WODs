# Prison Cell Circuit

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 25 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce reps or extend rest periods

**Workout:**
- 20 minutes AMRAP:
  - 20 prisoner squats
  - 15 decline push-ups (feet elevated on wall)
  - 25 walking lunges
  - 10 handstand push-ups (or pike push-ups)
  - 30 jumping jacks

**Notes:**
Inspired by confined space workouts. Count total rounds completed.
