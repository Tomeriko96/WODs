# Core Crusher Supreme

**Equipment:** None
**Time Cap:** 14 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce reps or modify movements to knee variations

**Workout:**
- 4 rounds for time:
  - 30 bicycle crunches
  - 25 Russian twists
  - 20 leg raises
  - 15 V-ups
  - 10 hollow body rocks

**Notes:**
Maintain hollow body position throughout. Control the movement, don't rush.
