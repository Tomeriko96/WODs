# Jungle Warfare Training

**Equipment:** Suspension trainer, kettlebell, rope
**Time Cap:** 24 minutes
**Scaling:** Modify to basic movements

**Workout:**
Survival training circuit:
- 6 rounds of 4 minutes each:
  - 45 seconds: Rope climbs/pulls
  - 45 seconds: Suspension trainer rows
  - 45 seconds: Kettlebell carry
  - 45 seconds: Bear crawls

**Notes:** Survive the jungle! Functional movements for real-world scenarios. Move like your life depends on it!
