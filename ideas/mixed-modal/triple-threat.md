# Triple Threat

**Equipment:** Kettlebell, pull-up bar, box (optional)
**Time Cap:** 20 minutes
**Scaling:** Use lighter weights, reduce rounds

**Workout:**
- 5 rounds for time:
  - 10 kettlebell swings
  - 10 pull-ups
  - 10 box jumps

**Notes:**
Rest as needed. Focus on explosive movement and good form.
