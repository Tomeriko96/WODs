# Total Body Tornado

**Equipment:** Kettlebell, box or step
**Time Cap:** 18 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce weight or modify movements

**Workout:**
- 21-15-9 reps for time:
  - Kettlebell swings
  - Box step-ups (total)
  - Push-ups
  - Sit-ups

**Notes:**
Choose challenging but sustainable weights. Keep transitions quick.
