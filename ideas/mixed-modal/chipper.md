# Chipper

**Equipment:** Wall ball, pull-up bar
**Time Cap:** 25 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce reps or substitute exercises

**Workout:**
- For time:
  - 50 wall balls
  - 40 sit-ups
  - 30 burpees
  - 20 pull-ups
  - 10 sprints (20m each)

**Notes:**
Break up reps as needed. Modify pull-ups as needed.
