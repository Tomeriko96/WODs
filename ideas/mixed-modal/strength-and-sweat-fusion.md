# Strength and Sweat Fusion

**Equipment:** Barbell, kettlebell
**Time Cap:** 25 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce weights or rounds

**Workout:**
- 5 rounds for time:
  - 400m run (or 90 seconds cardio)
  - 10 deadlifts
  - 15 kettlebell goblet squats
  - 20 push-ups

**Notes:**
Use 70% of deadlift max. Maintain form as fatigue sets in.
