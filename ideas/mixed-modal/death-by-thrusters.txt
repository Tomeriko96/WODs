Death by Thrusters
Equipment: Barbell, dumbbells, or kettlebells
Time Cap: Until failure
Scaling: Use lighter weight or reduce reps per minute
Tags: mixed-modal, strength, cardio
Workout:
Minute 1: 1 thruster
Minute 2: 2 thrusters
Minute 3: 3 thrusters
Continue adding 1 rep each minute until failure
Notes: Choose a weight you can do 10+ unbroken reps with when fresh.
