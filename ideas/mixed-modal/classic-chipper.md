# Classic Chipper

**Equipment:** Pull-up bar
**Time Cap:** 20 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce reps by half, use assistance for pull-ups

**Workout:**
For time:
- 50 air squats
- 40 sit-ups
- 30 push-ups
- 20 burpees
- 10 pull-ups

**Notes:** Work through exercises in order. Break up movements as needed but try to maintain consistent pace.
