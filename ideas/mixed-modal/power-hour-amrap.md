# Power Hour AMRAP

**Equipment:** Dumbbells, kettlebell
**Time Cap:** 12 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce weights or reps per round

**Workout:**
- 12 minutes AMRAP:
  - 8 dumbbell man makers
  - 12 kettlebell swings
  - 16 air squats
  - 200m run (or 45 seconds cardio)

**Notes:**
Pace for consistency. Track total rounds and reps completed.
