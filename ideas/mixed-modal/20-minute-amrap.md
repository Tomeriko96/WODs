# 20-Minute AMRAP

**Equipment:** Kettlebell, box (or sturdy platform)
**Time Cap:** 20 minutes
**Scaling:** Use lighter kettlebell or step-ups instead of box jumps

**Workout:**
- AMRAP in 20 minutes:
  - 10 push-ups
  - 15 kettlebell swings
  - 20 box jumps

**Notes:**
Count total rounds and reps completed.
