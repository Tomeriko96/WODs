# Yoke Builder

**Equipment:** Barbell, dumbbells, pull-up bar
**Time Cap:** 20 minutes
**Scaling:** Use lighter weights, reduce reps

**Workout:**
- 4 rounds for quality:
  - 8 front squats
  - 8 bent-over rows
  - 8 pull-ups
  - 8 overhead presses

**Notes:**
Rest as needed. Focus on full range of motion and good form.
