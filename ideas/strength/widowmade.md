# Widowmade

**Equipment:** Barbell, dumbbells
**Time Cap:** 20 minutes
**Scaling:** Use lighter weights, reduce reps

**Workout:**
- Squat x 20
- Deadlift x 20
- Push-ups or bench x 20
- Press anything x 20

**Notes:**
After squat/deadlift widowmaker superset, push-ups and dumbbell pressing will be tough. Use light dumbbells.
