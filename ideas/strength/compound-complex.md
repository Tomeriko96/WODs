# Compound Complex

**Equipment:** Barbell, box
**Time Cap:** 28 minutes
**Scaling:** Reduce weights or simplify complexes

**Workout:**
- 4 rounds:
  - 5 front squats
  - 5 push press
  - 5 back squats
  - 5 good mornings
  - Rest 90 seconds
- Use same barbell throughout

**Notes:**
Choose weight based on weakest lift. Don't let bar touch ground during complex.
