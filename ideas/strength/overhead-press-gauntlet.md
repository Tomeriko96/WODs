# Overhead Press Gauntlet

**Equipment:** Barbell or dumbbells
**Time Cap:** 15 minutes
**Scaling:** Use lighter weights, reduce rounds

**Workout:**
- 5 rounds for time:
  - 10 overhead presses
  - 10 push-ups
  - 10 sit-ups

**Notes:**
Rest as needed. Focus on strict press form and full range of motion.
