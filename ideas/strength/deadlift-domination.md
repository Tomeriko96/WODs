# Deadlift Domination

**Equipment:** Barbell
**Time Cap:** 25 minutes
**Scaling:** Use lighter weights or deficit/rack pulls

**Workout:**
- Deadlift ladder: 1-2-3-4-5-4-3-2-1
- Rest 60 seconds between sets
- Use 80-85% of 1RM
- Finish: 2 x 10 Romanian deadlifts at 60%

**Notes:**
Maintain perfect form throughout. Add weight if feeling strong.
